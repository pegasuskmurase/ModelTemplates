Data files of neutrino and gamma-ray spectra for NGC 1068, NGC 4151, NGC 7469, CGCG 420-015 and Circinus [CITATION: Carpio, Kheirandish & Murase, arXiv:2603.08684]

The columns are as follows:

Energy: E [GeV]
Gamma-ray flux dN_gamma/dE [GeV^-1 cm^-2 s^-1] w.o. EBL
Neutrino flux dN_neutrino/dE [GeV^-1 cm^-2 s^-1] generated neutrinos (all flavors)

E^2 dN/dE  gives the differential flux at a source distance of 1 Mpc, only data above 1 MeV may be used.

Each source has two .dat files. For example, NGC1068.dat contains the fluxes for the MCMC median parameters in the left panel of Figure 1. On the other hand, NGC168_MeanCurve.dat is the mean curve generated through the modified band depth approach, and will match the red and blue lines in the right panel of Figure 1. The differences between both curves are minor.
